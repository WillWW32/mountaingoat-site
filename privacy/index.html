Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Mountain Goats Documentary ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website mountaingoats.co and The Collective Hub. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and other contact details you provide when joining The Collective or signing agreements.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with the site, including pages visited and access times.
  • Session Data: Access codes and session identifiers stored locally in your browser to maintain your login state.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to The Collective Hub and its features (announcements, shoots, documents, agreements).
  • To send SMS notifications about shoots, events, and production updates via our messaging system.
  • To manage participation in the documentary including agreements, RSVP tracking, and scheduling.
  • To improve the site experience and functionality.

3. SMS Communications

If you provide your phone number through our SMS opt-in form or as a Collective member, you may receive SMS messages related to production updates, shoot schedules, and announcements. Message frequency varies based on production activity. Standard message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of any purchase or participation in the documentary.

We will never share, sell, or provide your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number is used solely to send you production-related updates from Mountain Goats Documentary.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, SMS delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

The site uses browser session storage to maintain your login state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies. Basic analytics may use first-party cookies for site improvement purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of SMS communications.

8. Children's Privacy

The Collective Hub involves participants who may be minors. Parental or guardian consent is obtained for all minor participants as part of the documentary agreement process.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Mountain Goats production team through The Collective Hub or at the contact information provided during onboarding.
